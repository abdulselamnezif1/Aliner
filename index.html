Ethiopia's Top Attractions

Rock-Hewn Churches of Lalibela

11 monolithic churches carved out of rock in the 12th century, often called the "Eighth Wonder of the World".

Semien Mountains National Park

A UNESCO World Heritage Site with dramatic landscapes, endemic wildlife, and Africa's highest peak.

Denakil Depression

One of the hottest and lowest places on Earth, with colorful hydrothermal fields and salt plains.

Omo Valley Tribes

Home to diverse indigenous cultures with unique traditions, body painting, and rituals.
